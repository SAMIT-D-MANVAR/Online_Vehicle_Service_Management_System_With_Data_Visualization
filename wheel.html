Wheel Care Service

The wheels of your car need regular attention and care. Wheels get damaged due to bad roads and pot holes leading to misalignment. This affects the drive and safety on the roads. We at AUTODOC help you maximize the life of your car wheels by offering a wide array of wheel care services.

We specialise in car wheel alignment service wherein our experts use latest technology and best equipment to check the alignment and fix it. Appropriate car wheel alignment ensures the durability of the wheels, a smooth and safe drive and major savings on fuel.

Apart from wheel alignment service, we also do car wheel balancing, car wheel repairs and replacements, fix punctures and damaged valves for tubeless tyres, perform tyre rotation, check air pressure, adjust inflation and fix all other problems related to the wheels of the vehicle. We all know that wheels are expensive.

Importance Of Wheel Alignment

It is of utmost importance that car owners get wheel alignment checks to prolong the life of their car’s tyres as much as possible. Misaligned tyres are much more likely to premature wear and tear. This in turn forces you to change your tyres in frequent intervals, adding unnecessary costs. You know that your car require wheel alignment if the car does not move in a straight line when you aren’t holding the steering wheel. If you notice this to be the case in your car, contact our AUTODOC Services outlet immediately for wheel alignment done with the highest precision. Inappropriate alignment of the wheels can also lead to other problems in your car such as unusual vibrations while driving and problems with suspension.

Importance Of Wheel Balancing

MFCS technicians recommend tyre balancing as soon as you get new tyres for your car. This would ensure that all of the tyres have equal wheel weights. You’ll know if your car requires wheel balancing if your steering wheel is rocking back and forth as you drive. If you notice this in your car, contact the AUTODOC Services, as driving with wheels that require balancing can be very dangerous.

You can also tell if your car requires wheel balancing if certain tyres are wearing out at different rates than the others. The vibrations caused by imbalanced wheels can also put pressure on the lower ball joints, axles and other crucial parts of your car. The increased pressure can also cause the wear and tear rate of these parts to increase, adding to maintenance problems of your car.
